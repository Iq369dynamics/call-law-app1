Employment Rights

Millions of workers lose rights they're entitled to — every year.

calllawapp.com  ·  24/7 Attorney Access

Employment violations are widespread — and most go unchallenged because workers don't know what the law protects. Here's what every worker should know:

  • ⚖️ Wrongful termination is real."At-will" employment doesn't let employers fire you for discriminatory or retaliatory reasons — that's illegal under federal law.
  • 📄 Never sign severance blindly.Severance and non-compete agreements can permanently waive rights you're legally entitled to keep. Get counsel first.
  • 🔗 Non-competes may not be enforceable.Many non-compete clauses are overly broad or unenforceable. An attorney can tell you where you actually stand.
  • ⏱️ Deadlines can kill your claim.EEOC charges must be filed within 180–300 days of a violation. Missing the window means losing your right to act.
